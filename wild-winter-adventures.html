Wild Winter Adventures

Wrap up this Winter and discover a number of amazing animal experiences as you journey through Chessington Zoo and SEA LIFE centre with a Wild Winter Adventure. Then warm up at the Chessington Safari Hotel or Chessington Azteca Hotel where you can continue your Wild Adventure.

Please note, Chessington World of Adventures theme park is closed during this time.

Your package will include:

  • A visit to the Zoo and SEA LIFE centre
  • Monkey around in the Savannah Splash kids water play area
  • Meet some cuddly creatures with Animal Meet & Greets
  • Watch some great family movies during Movie Time!
  • Enjoy kids evening entertainment with The Rangers Club
  • Meet some friendly faces from Madagascar
  • Eat a really wild breakfast the next morning with some extra treats

Itinerary:

  • 3.00: Meet one of our friends from Penguins of Madagascar.
  • 4.00: Get up close and personal to some cuddly critters with Animal Meet & Greets.
  • 5.00: Pips Adventure - Meet Pip, one of our larger than life Hotel residents. Join him in this fun interactive story.
  • 5.30: Fun and Games with Jungle Jerry - Join in with these fun kids games from around the World.
  • 6.00: Time to Shine Show - A fun, vibrant and exciting show that gets the kids up on their feet and ready to boogie. We make the children the stars of the show as they strut their stuff and demonstrate each of their individual talents.
  • 6.30: Pip the Magic Meerkat - Make friends with Pip. He’s a meerkat and not just any old meerkat, Pip has magical powers. Join him in this wild adventure and dreams come true as you become what you’ve always wanted to be!
  • 7.00: Family Game Show - Now’s the time to get the mums and dads involved, so let’s see your best dance moves as you join in the family fun, in this all inclusive game show.
  • 7.30: Children’s Disco - Boogie and dance along to your favourite party songs.
  • 8.30: Gruffalo Bedtime Story - Listen to your favourite Gruffalo tales, read by our fun and energetic Rangers.

Wild Winter Adventures is valid on these dates

  • 1st — 3rd, 9th, 10th, 16th, 17th, 23rd, 24th, 30th and 31st January
  • 6th, 7th, 27th and 28th February
  • 6th and 7th March
