General

When can I check in and check out?

The earliest check in time is 3pm, and the latest check out time is 11am. Please note you can check in at the hotel any time after 3pm, you are not required to check in at the hotel upon your arrival to the park.

Can I leave my luggage at the hotel?

There is a luggage drop service available at the Chessington Azteca Hotel.

Is there a pool?

Yes, there is a splash play zone located within Savannah Splash, as well as a swimming pool, sauna, whirlpool and gym.

Where can I park?

There is free on site parking available to all guests.

What is there for the kids to do?

Children will love staying at the Azteca Hotel right next to Chessington World of Adventures. Fun filled activities include:
• Savannah Splash water play zone
• Entertainment from the Rangers Club
• Movie Club!
• Animal Meet and Greets
• Exclusive evening access to Wanyama Village and Reserve (April to mid September)
• Get up close to real life monkeys in the AMAZU Treetop Adventure
• PLUS - meet some of the stars from Madagascar!
• Character breakfasts with dressed up staff

What facilities are there for babies/infants?

Little ones are well cared for at the Azteca Hotel. Facilities for babies and infants include:

What can I get for breakfast?

A Full English Breakfast is included as part of your package.

What's the restaurant like?

The Temple Restaurant and Bar is a unique and interactive premium dining experience set in the heart of a mystical ancient temple, where a fountain that was once the life force of the jungle is protected by the wildlife that call it home. The Temple Restaurant and Bar is open for breakfast from 6:30am to 10:30am and for dinner between 6:30pm and 9:30pm (5:30pm and 9:30pm on weekends and school holidays).

Are there kids dining options?

The Temple Restaurant & Bar is the perfect place for your little ones to eat dinner with a difference.

Is there room service?

Room service is available 24 hours a day at the Chessington Azteca Hotel.

Are there any dining offers/promotions?

There are currently no dining offers or promotions.

Rooms

How is this room laid out?

All rooms can sleep up to a maximum of 5 people, and will have a double bed, and a separate children's sleeping area consisting of a bunk bed with a pull out bed.

Map

How do I get there?

Chessington World of Adventures is situated on the A243, two miles from the A3 and M25 (junction 9 or 10). The Park is well signposted.

If you're arriving by rail, the nearest train station is Chessington South Station, which is approximately a 10 minute walk from the Park.

How do I get around?

This hotel is situated on the resort, so there's no need to travel to the Park.
